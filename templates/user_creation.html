Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our ChatManip. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. By using our services, you consent to the practices described herein.

Information We Collect

We collect and store certain information to enhance your experience and improve our services. This includes:

  • Chat logs: We may save chat logs to analyze and improve user interactions. These logs are stored anonymously and are not linked to identifiable personal information.
  • Demographic Information: We collect age, gender, and occupation to better understand our user base. This information is anonymized and aggregated for statistical analysis.
  • IP Address: We may collect your IP address for security purposes and to analyze usage patterns. IP addresses are stored in an anonymized format.
  • Language Preference: We record your language preference for tailored user experiences. This information is kept separate from your personal data.
  • Survey Answers: If you participate in surveys, we may collect your responses anonymously to gain insights into user opinions and preferences.
  • Metadata: We collect metadata, such as timestamps, to analyze usage patterns and improve our services. This data is anonymized and used for statistical purposes.

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used to enhance and personalize our services. We analyze anonymized information to improve user experiences, troubleshoot technical issues, and develop new features. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties in a personally identifiable manner.

Data Security

We take data security seriously. We use industry-standard measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Your Choices

You can choose not to provide certain information or opt-out of data collection. However, please note that this might affect your experience and limit the availability of certain features.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please review it periodically for any changes.

Contact Us
